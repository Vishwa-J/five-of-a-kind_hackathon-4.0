Umeed

We are a group of open-source contributors that built the website Umeed with the goal of offering simple methods to improve mental health.

Contact Us

Anxiety and depression are the most common mental illnesses.

There is no one-size-fits-all approach to mental health treatment, as every person's experience and needs are unique.

Mental health can be improved by self-care practices such as exercise, good nutrition, and sleep hygiene.
